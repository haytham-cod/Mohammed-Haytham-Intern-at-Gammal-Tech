Over the past days during my internship at Gammal Tech, I’ve been sharing what I’ve been learning about APIs.

To conclude this short series, here is the big idea:

APIs are one of the core technologies that make modern software possible.

Instead of building every feature from scratch, developers integrate different services through APIs.

This allows applications to:
• scale faster
• connect multiple systems
• deliver richer functionality

From mobile apps to cloud platforms, APIs are the bridges that allow digital systems to collaborate and evolve.

This internship has already been a great opportunity to deepen my understanding of how modern technology works, and I’m excited to keep learning and building.
